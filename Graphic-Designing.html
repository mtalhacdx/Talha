Frequently Asked Questions

What design services do you offer?

I offer a comprehensive range of design services including logo design, brand identity systems, print materials (business cards, brochures, flyers), digital graphics (social media, web banners), packaging design, and more. Each project is customized to meet your specific needs.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive all standard file formats including high-resolution print-ready files (PDF, EPS, AI) and web-optimized files (PNG, JPG, SVG). I also provide source files upon request so you have full flexibility for future modifications.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! All my design packages include unlimited revisions, so you can request as many changes as needed until you are fully satisfied with the final result.

How do we get started?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact me through the contact form or email, and we'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your project requirements, timeline, and budget. From there, I'll provide a detailed proposal and we can begin the creative process.
